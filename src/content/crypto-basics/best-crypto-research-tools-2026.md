Good research separates successful crypto investors from gamblers. Here are the essential tools for evaluating projects, tracking markets, and finding opportunities.

## Market Data & Discovery

| Tool | Best For | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| CoinGecko | General research, trust score, developer data | Free |
| CoinMarketCap | Price tracking, rankings, exchange data | Free |
| Messari | Professional research, reports | Free + Pro ($30/mo) |
| The Block | News, research, data dashboards | Free + Pro |
| Nansen | Smart money tracking, wallet labels | Pro ($150+/mo) |
| Dune Analytics | Custom on-chain queries, dashboards | Free + Pro |
| DeFiLlama | DeFi TVL, yields, protocol data | Free |
| Token Terminal | Fundamental crypto metrics (P/E, revenue) | Free + Pro |

## Fundamental Analysis

### Tokenomics & Supply
Token Unlocks — Track vesting schedules and token unlocks
CoinGecko — Circulating supply, market cap, fully diluted valuation

### Smart Contract Analysis
Etherscan — Verify contracts, read functions, check holders
Solscan — Solana blockchain explorer
RugCheck — Token safety score for Solana tokens
TokenSniffer — Token analysis for Ethereum tokens

### Wallet Tracking
Etherscan labels — Identify known wallets (exchange, scam, whale)
Arkham Intelligence — Entity-labeled wallet dashboard
Bubblemaps — Visual holder distribution

## The Research Workflow

### 1. Screen (5 minutes)
Use CoinGecko to shortlist projects by:
- Market cap (top 200)
- Category (DeFi, AI, L1, etc.)
- Recent gainers/losers

### 2. Analyze Fundamentals (30 minutes)
- Tokenomics (supply schedule, inflation, distribution)
- Team (doxxed? track record?)
- Code (GitHub activity, audits)
- Revenue (does the protocol make money?)

### 3. On-Chain Check (15 minutes)
- Holder distribution (Bubblemaps)
- Smart money activity (Nansen/Arkham)
- TVL trend (DeFiLlama)
- Volume and liquidity (DexScreener)

### 4. Community Check (10 minutes)
- Twitter engagement (real or bots?)
- Discord/Telegram activity
- Sentiment (positive or desperate?)
- BitcoinTalk discussion

### 5. Risk Assessment (5 minutes)
- Audit status
- Liquidity lock
- Team vesting schedule
- Regulatory risk

## Verdict
The best research toolkit for most people: CoinGecko (market data), Token Unlocks (supply schedule), DeFiLlama (protocol stats), Bubblemaps (holders), and DexScreener (trading data). Start with free tools and upgrade to paid tools as your portfolio grows.
