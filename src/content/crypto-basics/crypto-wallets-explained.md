A crypto wallet is how you store, send, and receive cryptocurrency. Choosing the right wallet is one of the most important decisions in crypto.

Here's everything you need to know.

## The Golden Rule

**Not your keys, not your crypto.**

If someone else holds your private keys, they control your crypto. If the exchange fails (like FTX), your money is gone.

## Wallet Types

### Hot Wallets (Online)

Always connected to the internet. Convenient but less secure.

| Wallet | Type | Best For |
|--------|------|---------|
| **MetaMask** | Browser extension | DeFi, NFTs |
| **Trust Wallet** | Mobile app | All-around |
| **Phantom** | Browser extension | Solana ecosystem |
| **Coinbase Wallet** | Mobile app | Beginners |

**Pros:**
- Easy to use
- Free
- Instant access
- Good for small amounts

**Cons:**
- Connected to internet (hackable)
- Less secure for large amounts
- Can lose funds if device is compromised

### Cold Wallets (Offline)

Physical devices that store keys offline. Most secure option.

| Wallet | Price | Best For |
|--------|-------|---------|
| **Ledger Nano S** | $79 | Budget option |
| **Ledger Nano X** | $149 | Bluetooth, more storage |
| **Trezor One** | $69 | Open source |
| **Trezor Model T** | $219 | Touchscreen, premium |

**Pros:**
- Keys never touch internet
- Immune to online hacks
- Best for large amounts
- You control everything

**Cons:**
- Costs $50-200
- Less convenient
- Can lose the device (but can recover with seed phrase)
- Slight learning curve

## Custodial vs Non-Custodial

### Custodial (Exchange Holds Keys)

| Exchange | Risk Level |
|----------|-----------|
| **Coinbase** | Low (US regulated) |
| **Kraken** | Low (US regulated) |
| **Binance** | Medium (offshore) |
| **Smaller exchanges** | High |

**When to use:** Small amounts, active trading

**Risk:** Exchange can fail, get hacked, or freeze your account

### Non-Custodial (You Hold Keys)

| Wallet | Control |
|--------|---------|
| **MetaMask** | Full control |
| **Ledger** | Full control |
| **Trezor** | Full control |

**When to use:** Long-term holding, DeFi, large amounts

**Benefit:** Nobody can take your crypto

<aside class="callout warning">
⚠️ <strong>FTX lesson:</strong> Customers lost $8 billion when FTX collapsed. If your crypto is on an exchange, it's not truly yours. Move large amounts to a self-custody wallet.
</aside>

## Recommended Setup

| Amount | Wallet Type |
|--------|------------|
| **Under $1,000** | Exchange (Coinbase, Kraken) |
| **$1,000-$10,000** | Hot wallet (MetaMask, Trust) |
| **$10,000+** | Cold wallet (Ledger, Trezor) |
| **$100,000+** | Multi-sig cold wallet |

## How to Set Up a Wallet

### MetaMask (Hot Wallet)

1. Install browser extension or mobile app
2. Create new wallet
3. Write down 12-word seed phrase (NEVER share this)
4. Set password
5. Done — you now control your crypto

### Ledger (Cold Wallet)

1. Buy from official website (never eBay)
2. Connect to computer
3. Follow setup instructions
4. Write down 24-word seed phrase
5. Transfer crypto from exchange

## Security Tips

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Write down seed phrase on paper | Store seed phrase digitally |
| Use strong, unique password | Reuse passwords |
| Enable 2FA on exchanges | Share private keys |
| Buy hardware wallet from official site | Buy from eBay/Amazon |
| Test with small amounts first | Send large amounts without testing |

<aside class="callout tip">
💡 <strong>Your seed phrase is everything.</strong> If someone gets your seed phrase, they get your crypto. Write it on paper, store it in a safe place, and NEVER enter it on any website.
</aside>
