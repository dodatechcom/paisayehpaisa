Market cap is the most important number in crypto. It tells you the true size and value of a cryptocurrency — not just its price.

Here's how it works and why it matters.

<img loading="lazy" src="/images/crypto-market-cap.svg" alt="Crypto market cap explained — formula, categories, and why it matters" style="width:100%; max-width:760px; height:auto; margin:1.5rem 0; border-radius:8px;" />

## The Formula

**Market Cap = Price x Circulating Supply**

| Crypto | Price | Supply | Market Cap |
|--------|-------|--------|-----------|
| Bitcoin | $65,000 | 19.5M | $1.27 trillion |
| Ethereum | $3,500 | 120M | $420 billion |
| Solana | $220 | 430M | $95 billion |
| Dogecoin | $0.15 | 143B | $21.5 billion |

<aside class="callout info">
📌 <strong>Price doesn't tell you value.</strong> A coin at $0.01 could be worth more than a coin at $1,000 if it has 100 billion more coins in supply. Market cap shows the real picture.
</aside>

## Why Market Cap Matters

### 1. Size = Stability

| Category | Market Cap | Volatility | Risk |
|----------|-----------|------------|------|
| **Large Cap** | $10B+ | Lower | Lower |
| **Mid Cap** | $1B-$10B | Medium | Medium |
| **Small Cap** | Under $1B | High | High |

### 2. Growth Potential

| Category | Potential | Example |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| **Large Cap** | 2-5x | BTC, ETH |
| **Mid Cap** | 3-10x | SOL, AVAX |
| **Small Cap** | 10-100x | New projects |

### 3. Risk Assessment

| Category | Downside Risk | Example Crash |
|----------|--------------|---------------|
| **Large Cap** | -50 to -80% | BTC 2022 |
| **Mid Cap** | -70 to -90% | SOL 2022 |
| **Small Cap** | -90 to -100% | Many go to zero |

## How to Use Market Cap

### Investment Decisions

| Goal | Market Cap Strategy |
|------|-------------------|
| **Safety** | Stick to large cap (BTC, ETH) |
| **Growth** | Mix large + mid cap |
| **Speculation** | Small portion in small cap |
| **Diversification** | Spread across all three |

### Comparing Cryptos

| Comparison | Price | Market Cap |
|-----------|-------|-----------|
| **BTC vs ETH** | BTC higher | BTC 3x larger |
| **SOL vs ADA** | SOL higher | SOL 5x larger |
| **DOGE vs SHIB** | DOGE higher | DOGE 10x larger |

<aside class="callout warning">
⚠️ <strong>Beware of low-price traps.</strong> A coin at $0.001 is not "cheaper" than a coin at $100. Look at market cap, not price, to understand value.
</aside>

## Summary

| Key Point | Takeaway |
|-----------|----------|
| **Formula** | Price x Supply = Market Cap |
| **Large Cap** | $10B+, stable, lower risk |
| **Mid Cap** | $1B-$10B, growth potential |
| **Small Cap** | Under $1B, high risk/high reward |
| **Don't compare prices** | Compare market caps |
| **Market cap = true value** | Not price per coin |

<aside class="callout tip">
💡 <strong>Always check market cap before investing.</strong> A coin with a $100M market cap has 10x more room to grow than one with a $1B market cap — but also 10x more risk of going to zero.
</aside>
