Crypto moves in cycles: bull markets (prices rising) and bear markets (prices falling). Understanding these cycles is the key to building wealth in crypto.

Here's how to spot them and what to do.

## Bull Market (Uptrend)

### Definition
Prices rise 20%+ from recent lows and continue climbing.

### Characteristics
- Higher highs and higher lows
- Optimistic sentiment
- FOMO buying
- Media hype
- "Everyone is making money"

### What to Do
| Strategy | Action |
|----------|--------|
| **Hold** | Don't sell too early |
| **Take profits** | Sell 10-20% at each new high |
| **Don't FOMO** | Don't buy at the top |
| **Set targets** | Know when to exit |

## Bear Market (Downtrend)

### Definition
Prices fall 20%+ from recent highs and continue declining.

### Characteristics
- Lower highs and lower lows
- Fear and panic selling
- Negative news cycle
- "Bitcoin is dead" headlines
- Everyone says "it's over"

### What to Do
| Strategy | Action |
|----------|--------|
| **Don't panic sell** | Selling at the bottom is the worst mistake |
| **Dollar-cost average** | Buy regularly at lower prices |
| **Rebalance** | Buy more at discounts |
| **Stay calm** | Every bear market has recovered |

## The 4-Year Crypto Cycle

Crypto follows a roughly 4-year pattern tied to Bitcoin halving events:

| Phase | Year | What Happens | Strategy |
|-------|------|-------------|----------|
| **Accumulation** | Year 1 | Prices bottom, smart money buys | Buy aggressively |
| **Mark-Up** | Year 2-3 | Prices rise, attention grows | Hold and add |
| **Distribution** | Peak | Euphoria, smart money sells | Take profits |
| **Mark-Down** | Year 4 | Bear market, panic selling | Prepare to buy |

### Historical Cycles

| Cycle | Peak | Bottom | Recovery |
|-------|------|--------|----------|
| 2013-2017 | $1,100 | $200 | 5.5x |
| 2017-2021 | $20,000 | $3,200 | 6.25x |
| 2021-2025 | $69,000 | $15,500 | 4.5x |

<aside class="callout info">
📌 <strong>Every bear market has been followed by a new bull market.</strong> The pattern has repeated 4 times. The key is surviving the bear market to profit from the next bull run.
</aside>

## How to Spot Market Tops

| Signal | What It Means |
|--------|--------------|
| **Euphoria everywhere** | Taxi drivers talking about crypto |
| **New all-time highs daily** | FOMO is extreme |
| **Leverage at record highs** | Too much borrowing |
| **Meme coins pumping** | Speculation is rampant |
| **"This time is different"** | It never is |

## How to Spot Market Bottoms

| Signal | What It Means |
|--------|--------------|
| **"Bitcoin is dead" headlines** | Maximum fear |
| **90%+ decline from highs** | Capitulation complete |
| **Miners shutting down** | Weak hands out |
| **Low volume** | No buyers left |
| **Everyone says "never again"** | Best time to buy |

## The Strategy: Buy Fear, Sell Greed

| Market Phase | Sentiment | Action |
|-------------|-----------|--------|
| **Extreme fear** | "It's going to zero" | Buy aggressively |
| **Fear** | "I'm worried" | Dollar-cost average |
| **Neutral** | "Let's wait and see" | Hold positions |
| **Greed** | "I'm making money" | Start taking profits |
| **Extreme greed** | "It'll never stop" | Sell most positions |

## Summary

| Key Point | Takeaway |
|-----------|----------|
| **Bull market** | Prices rise 20%+, hold and take profits |
| **Bear market** | Prices fall 20%+, buy and don't panic |
| **4-year cycle** | Follows Bitcoin halving |
| **Buy fear** | Best returns come from buying bottoms |
| **Sell greed** | Best protection comes from selling tops |
| **Every cycle** | Bear markets always recover |

<aside class="callout tip">
💡 <strong>The simple strategy:</strong> Buy during bear markets when everyone is scared. Sell during bull markets when everyone is greedy. That's it. That's the whole strategy.
</aside>
