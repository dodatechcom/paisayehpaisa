Insurance works differently in every country. The US relies on private insurance, the UK has the NHS, and Canada has provincial health plans.

Here's how they compare across all major insurance types.

<img src="/images/insurance-comparison-us-uk-ca.svg" alt="Insurance comparison between US, UK, and Canada across health, auto, home, and life insurance" style="width:100%; max-width:760px; height:auto; margin:1.5rem 0; border-radius:8px;" />

## Health Insurance

| Factor | US | UK | Canada |
|--------|----|----|--------|
| **System** | Employer-based | NHS (public) | Provincial plans |
| **Cost** | $500-1,500/mo | Free (tax-funded) | Free (tax-funded) |
| **Coverage** | Varies by plan | Universal | Universal |
| **Wait times** | Short | Long | Medium |
| **Quality** | High (if insured) | Good | Good |
| **Prescription** | Varies | Low cost | Varies by province |

### Key Differences

| Country | System | Pros | Cons |
|---------|--------|------|------|
| **US** | Private | Short waits, best specialists | Expensive, complex |
| **UK** | NHS | Free, universal | Long waits, less choice |
| **Canada** | Provincial | Free, universal | Long waits, limited coverage |

<aside class="callout info">
📌 <strong>If you're moving to the US:</strong> Budget $500-1,500/month for health insurance. It's the biggest financial difference between the US and UK/Canada.
</aside>

## Auto Insurance

| Factor | US | UK | Canada |
|--------|----|----|--------|
| **Required by** | State law | Road Tax | Provincial law |
| **Average cost** | $1,800/yr | 500-800 GBP/yr | $1,500-2,500/yr |
| **Liability required** | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **No-fault states** | Some | No | Yes (most provinces) |
| **Claims process** | Varies by state | Standardized | Standardized |

### Cost Comparison (Annual)

| Coverage Level | US | UK | Canada |
|---------------|----|----|--------|
| Basic liability | $600-1,000 | 300-500 GBP | $1,200-1,800 |
| Full coverage | $1,500-2,500 | 600-1,200 GBP | $2,000-3,500 |
| With accidents | $2,500-4,000 | 1,000-2,000 GBP | $3,000-5,000 |

## Homeowners Insurance

| Factor | US | UK | Canada |
|--------|----|----|--------|
| **Required by** | Mortgage lenders | Mortgage lenders | Mortgage lenders |
| **Average cost** | $1,500-2,400/yr | 150-300 GBP/yr | $1,200-2,000/yr |
| **Flood coverage** | Separate | Separate | Separate |
| **Earthquake** | Separate | Rarely needed | Separate |
| **Contents included** | Yes | Separate (contents) | Yes |

## Life Insurance

| Factor | US | UK | Canada |
|--------|----|----|--------|
| **Market** | Private | Private | Private |
| **Term life cost** | $20-40/mo | 10-30 GBP/mo | $20-35/mo |
| **Whole life cost** | $250-400/mo | 100-250 GBP/mo | $200-350/mo |
| **Tax benefits** | Yes | Yes | Yes |

## Disability Insurance

| Factor | US | UK | Canada |
|--------|----|----|--------|
| **System** | Private/employer | Statutory + UC | CPP + private |
| **Employer coverage** | Often included | Statutory SSP | Varies |
| **Government program** | SSDI | Universal Credit | CPP Disability |
| **Private cost** | $25-100/mo | 20-50 GBP/mo | $25-80/mo |

## What You Need by Country

### If You Live in the US

| Insurance | Required? | Recommended? |
|-----------|----------|-------------|
| Health | No (but should have) | Yes |
| Auto | Yes (by state) | Full coverage |
| Home/Renters | If you own/rent | Yes |
| Life | No | If you have dependents |
| Disability | No | Yes |
| Umbrella | No | Yes ($1M+ coverage) |

### If You Live in the UK

| Insurance | Required? | Recommended? |
|-----------|----------|-------------|
| Health | NHS covers basics | Private for faster care |
| Auto | Yes (Road Tax) | Comprehensive |
| Home/Contents | If you own | Buildings + Contents |
| Life | No | If you have dependents |
| Income Protection | No | Yes |

### If You Live in Canada

| Insurance | Required? | Recommended? |
|-----------|----------|-------------|
| Health | Provincial plan | Yes (covers basics) |
| Auto | Yes (by province) | Full coverage |
| Home/Renters | If you own/rent | Yes |
| Life | No | If you have dependents |
| Disability | No | CPP + private |

## Cost of Living Impact

| Country | Annual Insurance Cost | % of Income |
|---------|----------------------|-------------|
| **US** | $8,000-15,000 | 10-15% |
| **UK** | £2,000-4,000 | 5-8% |
| **Canada** | $6,000-10,000 | 8-12% |

<aside class="callout warning">
⚠️ <strong>US insurance is expensive.</strong> Americans pay 2-3x more for health insurance than any other developed country. Budget accordingly if living in the US.
</aside>

## Summary

| Key Point | US | UK | Canada |
|-----------|----|----|--------|
| **Health** | Private, expensive | NHS, free | Provincial, free |
