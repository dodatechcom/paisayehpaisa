# Content Strategy

## Source
BitcoinTalk forums — daily monitoring of:
- Beginners & Help (~900k posts)
- Scam Accusations (~288k posts)
- Bitcoin Technical Support
- Local boards (Russian, Turkish, Indonesian)

## Format for each article
Title: [Question from BitcoinTalk]
Body: Explain in simple language, with visuals
Footer: "This question was asked on BitcoinTalk — see the original thread"

## Publishing cadence
1 article per day for first 30 days
Then 3-5 per week

## Language strategy
Phase 1: English
Phase 2: Russian (4.8M BTC posts, low English proficiency)
Phase 3: Turkish (1.1M posts), Indonesian (1M posts)

## Types of content
1. **Question answers** — direct answers to repeating questions
2. **How-to guides** — step-by-step with screenshots
3. **Tool pages** — interactive calculators, checkers
4. **Glossary** — crypto terms in plain language
