Student discounts can save you hundreds of pounds every year, but many students only scratch the surface. This guide covers every major student discount available in the UK, how to access them, and how to save over £500 annually.

## How Student Discounts Work

Most student discounts require proof of your student status. In the UK, three main verification methods exist:

- **TOTUM card** (formerly NUS Extra) — the physical student discount card accepted in-store and online
- **UNiDAYS** — free digital student verification used by hundreds of online retailers
- **Student Beans** — another free digital platform for online student discounts

Some retailers accept a valid student ID card directly. Always carry your university ID and check before paying.

## What You Need

### TOTUM Card (Formerly NUS Extra)

The TOTUM card is the official UK student discount card. It costs £12 for one year or £22 for two years.

**What you get:**

- Discounts at hundreds of high-street and online retailers
- A free 4-year digital TOTUM card included
- 10% off at ASOS (often rises to 20% during promotions)
- Discounts at McDonald's, KFC, Pizza Hut, and Subway
- Cinema discounts at Odeon, Vue, and Cineworld
- 10–15% off at Co-op and select supermarkets
- Technology discounts at Apple, Dell, and HP
- Fashion discounts at New Look, Burton, Miss Selfridge, and更多

**How to get it:**

1. Go to totum.com
2. Verify your student status with your university email
3. Pay £12 for one year
4. Receive a digital card instantly and a physical card by post

At £12 per year, the TOTUM card pays for itself within a few purchases.

### UNiDAYS

UNiDAYS is free to join and verify. It is primarily used for online shopping discounts.

**How to sign up:**

1. Download the UNiDAYS app or visit unidays.com
2. Sign up with your university email address
3. Verify your student status
4. Browse available discounts or search for specific retailers

### Student Beans

Student Beans works similarly to UNiDAYS and is free to use.

**How to sign up:**

1. Download the Student Beans app or visit studentbeans.com
2. Create an account with your university email
3. Verify your student status
4. Access discounts online and in the app

## How to Verify Your Student Status

Most online discounts require digital verification. Here is how each platform works:

### UNiDAYS Verification

1. Create an account at unidays.com with your university email
2. UNiDAYS sends a verification email to your student address
3. Click the link to confirm — your account is verified instantly
4. Use the UNiDAYS app or website to browse and access discounts

### Student Beans Verification

1. Sign up at studentbeans.com with your university email
2. Student Beans sends a verification link to your student email
3. Click the link to complete verification
4. Access discounts through the app or website

### In-Store Verification

For physical stores, carry your:

- **University student ID card** — most retailers accept this
- **TOTUM card** — the official discount card
- **UNiDAYS or Student Beans app** — show your digital student status at the till

Always ask if a student discount is available, even if it is not advertised. Many retailers offer unadvertised discounts.

## Tips to Maximise Student Savings

### Always Ask

Many retailers do not advertise student discounts. Ask at checkout or check the retailer's website before paying. You might be surprised how many places offer unadvertised discounts.

### Show Your Card at Checkout

Carry your TOTUM card, student ID, or have the UNiDAYS app ready whenever you shop. Missing a 10% discount because you left your card at home is easy money lost.

### Use Your Student Email for Online Shopping

Many retailers verify student status through your university email address. Keep your student email active and check it regularly — it is your key to hundreds of online discounts.

### Stack Discounts

Combine student discounts with other savings:

- Use a student discount plus a cashback website (e.g., TopCashback or Quidco) for double savings
- Time purchases with seasonal sales (Black Friday, Boxing Day) and add your student discount on top
- Use a student discount code from UNiDAYS plus a promo code from a voucher website

### Check Expiry Dates

Student discount offers change frequently. Some are time-limited promotions while others are ongoing. Set reminders to check for new offers at the start of each term.

### Share With Flatmates

Some discounts work better in groups. Split a Domino's order with your flatmates and apply the student discount to the full order. Everyone benefits.

### Use University Resources

Beyond retail discounts, your university offers free resources that save real money:

- **Free Microsoft Office 365** — save £80+ per year
- **Free software** — Adobe Creative Cloud, MATLAB, SPSS, and more through university licences
- **Free printing** — most universities include a printing quota
- **Free library** — borrow books instead of buying them
- **Free career support** — CV reviews, interview practice, job fairs
- **Free counselling** — mental health support at no cost
