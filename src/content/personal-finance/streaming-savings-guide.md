Most UK households spend £30 to £60 per month on streaming services, adding up to £360 to £720 per year. A few simple changes can cut that bill by half or more.

## UK Streaming Cost Comparison

Here is what the major UK streaming services cost as of 2026:

| Service | Cheapest Plan | Standard Plan | Premium Plan |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Netflix | £4.99 (with ads) | £10.99 (Full HD) | £17.99 (4K, 4 screens) |
| Disney+ | £4.99 (with ads) | £7.99 (Full HD) | £11.99 (4K, 4 screens) |
| Sky NOW | £9.99 (Basic) | £15.99 (Entertainment) | £34.99 (Ultimate) |
| Amazon Prime Video | £8.99 (with Prime) | £8.99 (with Prime) | £8.99 (with Prime) |
| Apple TV+ | £8.99 | £8.99 | £8.99 |
| Paramount+ | £6.99 | £6.99 | £6.99 |

Prices are monthly unless stated. Most services offer a standard and premium tier with the main difference being video quality and the number of simultaneous screens.

## Share Family Plans

The single biggest saving comes from splitting the cost of a family plan with others. Most streaming services allow multiple simultaneous streams on their higher-tier plans.

### Netflix

The Standard plan at £10.99 allows two simultaneous screens. Split with a friend or family member and you each pay £5.50. The Premium plan at £17.99 allows four screens, so splitting four ways costs £4.50 each.

### Disney+

The Premium plan at £11.99 supports four simultaneous streams. Split four ways, that is £3.00 per person. Even splitting two ways on the standard plan at £7.99 gives you each £4.00.

### Amazon Prime Video

Amazon Prime at £8.99 per month includes free delivery, Prime Music, and Prime Reading alongside the video service. If you already use Amazon for shopping, the video is effectively very cheap. Sharing a household account means the cost is split across family members who benefit from all Prime perks.

## Switch to Annual Plans

Almost every streaming service offers a discount if you pay annually instead of monthly. The typical saving is around 15%, which equals nearly two months free.

| Service | Monthly Cost | Annual Cost | Annual Equivalent | Saving |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Netflix Standard | £10.99 | £107.88 | £8.99/month | £23.88/year |
| Disney+ Standard | £7.99 | £79.90 | £6.66/month | £15.98/year |
| Sky NOW Entertainment | £15.99 | £155.88 | £12.99/month | £36.00/year |

If you know you will use a service for the full year, annual billing is a straightforward saving. Set a reminder before renewal so you can decide whether to continue or switch to a different service.

## Cancel and Rotate

Instead of paying for three or four services simultaneously, keep one or two at a time and rotate between them every few months. Most major shows and films stay on a platform for months or years, so you rarely miss anything by waiting.

### How Rotation Works

1. Subscribe to Netflix and Disney+ for two months
2. Cancel both and switch to Sky NOW and Paramount+ for two months
3. Switch back or try something new

By rotating two services at a time, you always have fresh content without paying for everything at once.

### Annual Cost Comparison

| Approach | Monthly Cost | Annual Cost |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Netflix + Disney+ + Sky NOW (all year) | £28.97 | £347.64 |
| Rotating two services (4 switches per year) | ~£18.00 | ~£216.00 |
| **Saving** | | **~£131.64** |

If you add Amazon Prime, the savings reduce slightly since Prime includes other benefits, but the principle of rotating non-essential services still applies.

## Use Free Streaming Services

The UK has excellent free streaming options that many people overlook.

### Free UK Services

- **BBC iPlayer** - All BBC content including iPlayer exclusives. Free with a TV licence. Huge library of box sets, films, and live TV.
- **ITVX** - ITV's streaming service with free tier including all ITV shows. Premium tier removes ads but the free version is very usable.
- **Channel 4 (4oD)** - All Channel 4 programming available free with ads. Includes a large back catalogue.
- **My5** - Channel 5's free streaming service with on-demand content.
- **BBC Sounds** - Free radio and podcast app from the BBC.

### Free International Services

- **Tubi** - Large free library of films and TV shows with ads. No subscription required.
- **Pluto TV** - Free live TV channels and on-demand content. Hundreds of channels covering news, entertainment, and sport.
- **YouTube** - Free tier with ads. Many creators upload full-length content.
- **Kanopy** - Free with a UK library card. Offers films and documentaries.

Free services have ads, but they are a genuine alternative to paid subscriptions. Many people find they can replace one or two paid services with free options without noticing the difference.

## Haggle with Sky and Virgin Media

If you have Sky or Virgin Media bundles that include TV and streaming, you can often reduce the cost by calling their retention departments.

### Sky Retention Tips

1. Check your contract end date. You have more leverage once the minimum term is over.
2. Research what new customers pay for the same package.
3. Call Sky on 03442 41 41 41 and ask for the retentions department.
4. Explain you have found a cheaper deal elsewhere and ask what they can offer.
5. Sky often offers discounts of £10 to £20 per month or free upgrades to keep you.
6. Be prepared to actually cancel if the offer is not good enough. They may call back with a better deal.

### Virgin Media Retention Tips

1. Virgin Media prices rise annually by the rate of inflation plus 3.9%. Call after a price rise to negotiate.
2. Use the same approach as Sky: research competitors, call retentions, and be ready to leave.
3. Virgin often offers bill credits or reduced prices for 12 months.

## Bundle Wisely

Some services offer bundles that reduce the overall cost:

- **Disney+ and NOW** sometimes run joint promotions
- **Amazon Prime** includes video, music, and delivery for one price
- **Sky packages** bundle TV, broadband, and streaming at a lower combined cost than paying separately

Always calculate the total cost of a bundle versus paying for individual services. Bundles are not always cheaper.

## Key Takeaways

- Split family plans to halve or quarter your per-person cost
- Annual billing saves around 15% compared to monthly payments
- Rotating two services at a time instead of keeping all three saves over £130 per year
- BBC iPlayer, ITVX, Channel 4, Tubi, and Pluto TV offer substantial free content
- Call Sky or Virgin Media retentions to negotiate a lower price
- Review your subscriptions every few months and cancel what you do not watch regularly
