Bull vs Bear Market in Crypto: How to Spot and Profit From Each

June 16, 2026
🏷️ crypto-basics 🏷️ bull-market 🏷️ bear-market 🏷️ market-cycle

Crypto moves in cycles: bull markets (prices rising) and bear markets (prices falling). Understanding these cycles is the key to building wealth in crypto.

Here’s how to spot them and what to do.

Bull Market (Uptrend)

Definition

Prices rise 20%+ from recent lows and continue climbing.

Characteristics

  • Higher highs and higher lows
  • Optimistic sentiment
  • FOMO buying
  • Media hype
  • “Everyone is making money”

What to Do

StrategyAction
HoldDon’t sell too early
Take profitsSell 10-20% at each new high
Don’t FOMODon’t buy at the top
Set targetsKnow when to exit

Bear Market (Downtrend)

Definition

Prices fall 20%+ from recent highs and continue declining.

Characteristics

  • Lower highs and lower lows
  • Fear and panic selling
  • Negative news cycle
  • “Bitcoin is dead” headlines
  • Everyone says “it’s over”

What to Do

StrategyAction
Don’t panic sellSelling at the bottom is the worst mistake
Dollar-cost averageBuy regularly at lower prices
RebalanceBuy more at discounts
Stay calmEvery bear market has recovered

The 4-Year Crypto Cycle

Crypto follows a roughly 4-year pattern tied to Bitcoin halving events:

PhaseYearWhat HappensStrategy
AccumulationYear 1Prices bottom, smart money buysBuy aggressively
Mark-UpYear 2-3Prices rise, attention growsHold and add
DistributionPeakEuphoria, smart money sellsTake profits
Mark-DownYear 4Bear market, panic sellingPrepare to buy

📌 Every bear market has been followed by a new bull market. The pattern has repeated 4 times. The key is surviving the bear market to profit from the next bull run.

The Strategy: Buy Fear, Sell Greed

Market PhaseSentimentAction
Extreme fear”It’s going to zero”Buy aggressively
Fear”I’m worried”Dollar-cost average
Neutral”Let’s wait and see”Hold positions
Greed”I’m making money”Start taking profits
Extreme greed”It’ll never stop”Sell most positions

Summary

Key PointTakeaway
Bull marketPrices rise 20%+, hold and take profits
Bear marketPrices fall 20%+, buy and don’t panic
4-year cycleFollows Bitcoin halving

💡 The simple strategy: Buy during bear markets when everyone is scared. Sell during bull markets when everyone is greedy. That's it. That's the whole strategy.
