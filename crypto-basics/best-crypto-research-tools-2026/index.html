The Research Workflow

1. Screen (5 minutes)

Use CoinGecko to shortlist projects by:

  • Market cap (top 200)
  • Category (DeFi, AI, L1, etc.)
  • Recent gainers/losers

2. Analyze Fundamentals (30 minutes)

  • Tokenomics (supply schedule, inflation, distribution)
  • Team (doxxed? track record?)
  • Code (GitHub activity, audits)
  • Revenue (does the protocol make money?)

3. On-Chain Check (15 minutes)

  • Holder distribution (Bubblemaps)
  • Smart money activity (Nansen/Arkham)
  • TVL trend (DeFiLlama)
  • Volume and liquidity (DexScreener)

4. Community Check (10 minutes)

  • Twitter engagement (real or bots?)
  • Discord/Telegram activity
  • Sentiment (positive or desperate?)
  • BitcoinTalk discussion

5. Risk Assessment (5 minutes)

  • Audit status
  • Liquidity lock
  • Team vesting schedule
  • Regulatory risk

Verdict

The best research toolkit for most people: CoinGecko (market data), Token Unlocks (supply schedule), DeFiLlama (protocol stats), Bubblemaps (holders), and DexScreener (trading data). Start with free tools and upgrade to paid tools as your portfolio grows.
