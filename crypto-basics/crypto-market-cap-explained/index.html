Crypto Market Cap Explained: What It Means and Why It Matters

June 16, 2026
🏷️ crypto-basics 🏷️ market-cap 🏷️ beginner

Market cap is the most important number in crypto. It tells you the true size and value of a cryptocurrency — not just its price.

Here’s how it works and why it matters.

The Formula

Market Cap = Price x Circulating Supply

CryptoPriceSupplyMarket Cap
Bitcoin$65,00019.5M$1.27 trillion
Ethereum$3,500120M$420 billion
Solana$220430M$95 billion
Dogecoin$0.15143B$21.5 billion
📌 Price doesn't tell you value. A coin at $0.01 could be worth more than a coin at $1,000 if it has 100 billion more coins in supply. Market cap shows the real picture.
