Scam URL Checker

Check if a crypto website is safe — or a known scam.

How it works: The checker compares the domain against a database of 500+ known scam and phishing sites collected from BitcoinTalk and crypto security reports. It also detects typosquatting (fake domains that look like real ones). Results are provided as a guide — always exercise caution and do your own research. No data is sent to any server; everything runs locally in your browser.
