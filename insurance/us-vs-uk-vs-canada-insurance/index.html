US vs UK vs Canada Insurance Systems Compared

June 16, 2026
🏷️ insurance 🏷️ comparison 🏷️ us 🏷️ uk 🏷️ canada

Insurance works differently in every country. The US relies on private insurance, the UK has the NHS, and Canada has provincial health plans.

Here’s how they compare across all major insurance types.

📌 If you're moving to the US: Budget $500-1,500/month for health insurance. It's the biggest financial difference between the US and UK/Canada.
